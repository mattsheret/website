CV

Apr 2018 - Present: Product Manager, Juno – Building out AI-powered conveyancing services for people buying, selling or remortgaging their home. Steered the team through the transition to remote working, as well as scaling up pre-pandemic.

Apr 2016 - Mar 2018: Writer and strategist, if – Designing new, ethical models for data use. Plenty of ghostwriting too. Initially worked for if as a freelancer before joining full time in January 2017.

May 2012 - Apr 2016: Writer and strategist, Government Digital Service – The bread-and butter here was ghostwriting and creative production, but I also co-wrote the first iteration of the Service Standard, Service Design Manual and the Performance Platform.

Apr 2010 - Apr 2012: Data Griot, Last.fm – Daily content for web and CBS-owned radio stations around listening data and trends. Also involved in new service development (content and conception).

Jan 2008 onwards: Freelance content and strategy development – Clients included Ford, Tui and Newspaper Club. I'm probably proudest of the portfolio I helped Buckley Williams write... shortly before they decided to call it a day.
