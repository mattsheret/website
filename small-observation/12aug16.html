The Bureau of Small Observation
(August 2016)

Me, again

I can feel my teeth in my mouth. Sometimes I get that, when the focus of the second coffee wears off and all my mind can do is wander. If this happened with, say, my tibia I’d freak right out. At least my teeth are on display.

I wonder if I’ve been grinding them? There’s no pain or tension, it’s just weird to notice them, you know?
