How I work

Whether you need a keynote speech written from scratch or a quick polish of a critical blog post, the journey is the same: listening, drafting, revising.

If you're faced with a blank sheet of paper I can help you thumbnail an early draft, using a quick interview to draw out essential points. If you need a second pair of eyes on a keynote address, I can support you over a couple of sessions to rehearse the deck and smooth it out.

Most of my work falls somewhere in between: working up notes, refining a draft with a client, and creating room for them to add the finishing touches.
